Membership Zone

Neurodiversity in Business (NiB) will recognise three main types of membership:


Community Partners

Community Partners For organisations who are ND Specialists (provide an ND related service), advocacy groups or charities


Corporates

Businesses or corporations who want to focus on building neuroinclusive workplaces inside their own business


Individual

For individuals in the ND community who are not representing a corporate or a community partner


Membership Includes

Monthly Workshops

Key topics/stories from journeys of select companies/individuals will be shared and debated. These will be both physical and online depending on the circumstances.

Half-Yearly

Conferences, to bring the “great and good” from the neurodivergent community together to aid collaboration and drive forward change.


Sharing of industry good practice

Supporting the development of educational materials for training of staff, including managers training, recruitment, employee retention.

Mentoring programmes

Where neurodivergent individuals will be able to seek advice on starting, running and developing their business, using advice from other experienced neurodivergent business owners; or for managers (both Neurodivergent and Neurotypical) considering how to best manage their teams.
